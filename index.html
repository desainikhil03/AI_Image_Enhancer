Help & FAQ

Find answers to common questions about our AI Image Enhancer

What is AI Image Enhancer?

AI Image Enhancer is a powerful tool that uses advanced machine learning algorithms to enhance and upscale images. It's specifically designed for processing metal images used in nuclear reactor research at BARC, helping to improve image quality and clarity.

How do I use the image enhancer?

1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping, clicking the upload button, or pasting from clipboard (Ctrl+V)
2. Adjust the enhancement level using the slider (1x to 4x)
3. Click "Enhance Image" to process
4. Use the comparison slider to see before/after results
5. Download your enhanced image

What image formats are supported?

We support all common image formats including JPG, PNG, JPEG, and TIFF. For best results with metal images, we recommend using high-quality TIFF or PNG formats.

What is the maximum image size?

For optimal performance, we recommend images up to 10MB. Larger images may take longer to process but will still work.

How does the enhancement level work?

The enhancement level (1x to 4x) determines the intensity of the enhancement process. Higher levels provide more dramatic improvements but may take longer to process. We recommend starting with level 2 for most metal images.
